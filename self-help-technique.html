Self-Care Techniques

"Taking care of yourself is the most powerful way to begin taking care of others."

Mindful Breathing

Focus on your breath. Deep, slow breaths can center you and ease tension within minutes.

Physical Exercise

Move your body with activities like jogging, yoga, or even stretching to boost your mood and energy.

Creative Expression

Paint, draw, sing, or write — creative outlets help process emotions and ignite joy.

Journaling

Write your thoughts down daily. Journaling clears your mind and reveals patterns and progress.

Connecting with Nature

Walk in a park, hike a trail, or just sit by a tree — nature heals, calms, and inspires.
